About Us

Over a Decade of Manpower Excellence

INFO RECRUITMENT comes with over a decade of experience in manpower consulting and recruitment. We understand that every company has its own culture, values and expectations of its employees.

Our mission is to provide clients with excellent recruitment services based on mutual trust, transparency and the highest professional standards driven by quality and cost realization. We are meticulous, well-informed, focused, and dedicated to the long-term success of our clients without compromising the careers of candidates.

India offers a tremendous pool of manpower. INFO RECRUITMENT has vast experience in sourcing and deploying the most competent workforce in all spheres to reputed clients in Gulf/GCC and Middle East countries.

How We Work

Our Recruitment Process

1

Client's Requirement

Receive demand letter and understand specific manpower requirements from client.

2

Advertisement & Database

Newspaper advertisement, database search, and placement network activation.

3

Shortlisting

Applications are shortlisted and chartered assessment done by industry experts.

4

Interview & Trade Test

Candidates called for interview and trade test by company delegates and experts.

5

Medical & Visa

Medically fit candidate documents are further processed for visa application.

6

Visa Stamping

Visa stamping, immigration clearance, and air ticketing completed.

7

Orientation

Candidates briefed on company culture and the destination country.

8

Deployment

Candidate successfully deployed to job location, ready to contribute.
